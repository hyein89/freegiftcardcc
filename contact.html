What to Include in Your Message

To help us process your request as quickly as possible, please follow these guidelines when sending your email:

1. Clear Subject Line: Briefly state the nature of your inquiry (e.g., "Verification Issue", "Partnership Inquiry", "General Question").
2. Detailed Description: Provide as much context as possible. If you experienced an error during the offer verification phase, please mention the specific offer name and the time of the occurrence.
3. Device Information: Let us know if you are accessing our platform via a mobile device, tablet, or desktop, as this helps our technical team replicate and fix any layout issues.
4. No Sensitive Data: For your security, never include sensitive personal information, passwords, or financial details in your emails to us. We will never ask for such information.

Business and Advertising Partnerships

We are always open to expanding our network. If you represent an advertising agency, an offer-wall provider (such as Monetag, Adsterra, etc.), or a digital brand looking to distribute promotional codes through our Content Locker system, please use the subject line "Partnership Inquiry". Our business development team prioritizes these communications and will provide our media kit and integration requirements.

Response Time

Our support desk operates Monday through Friday during standard business hours. While we strive to respond to all legitimate inquiries within 24 to 48 hours, please allow additional time during weekends or global holidays. We appreciate your patience and look forward to assisting you.
