Privacy Policy & Data Protection

Information We Collect

is designed to operate with maximum respect for user anonymity. We explicitly do not require account creation, nor do we host mandatory registration forms that ask for sensitive personally identifiable information (PII) such as your full name, physical address, or financial details.

However, to operate our Human Verification System and prevent automated bot abuse, we do collect specific non-identifying technical data. When you interact with our platform, our servers automatically log standard information provided by your web browser. This includes your Internet Protocol (IP) address, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and the number of clicks. This data is utilized strictly for analyzing trends, administering the site, tracking user movement on the website, and gathering broad demographic information for internal optimization.

Geo-Location and API Utilization

To provide you with the most relevant promotional offers within our Content Locker, utilizes secure third-party API services to detect your general geographic location (City and Country). This geo-targeting ensures that the digital rewards and verification tasks presented to you are valid and redeemable in your specific region. This location data is processed in real-time and is not permanently stored in our core databases.

Cookies and Web Beacons

Like any other professional website, uses "cookies". These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited. The information is used to optimize the users' experience by customizing our web page content based on visitors' browser type and/or other information.

In the context of our digital reward system, cookies are absolutely essential for tracking the completion status of third-party offers. When you click on an external promotional link, a temporary session cookie is generated to communicate with the advertiser's network. Once the verification task is successfully completed, this cookie sends a postback signal to our server, triggering the release of your requested digital content.
